Set a Benchmark!

Research suggests that benchmarking is a proven way to improve performances by facilitating best practises aimed at achieving quality improvements. By comparing performance metrics with industry leaders, organizations can identify gaps and areas for enhancement, leading to a better overall performance.

To assess your performance, Kathakali requires you to submit a piece of acting that you want to assess yourself against. This can be a scene from your favourite movie, a monologue found on YouTube or even a video of YOUR best performance. Whatever it is, please ensure that the subject's face is clearly visible at most times during the video.
